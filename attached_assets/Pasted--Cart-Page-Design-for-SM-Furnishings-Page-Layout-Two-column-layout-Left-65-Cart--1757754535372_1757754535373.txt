### Interactive Elements
- **Auto-save**: Cart updates saved automatically
- **Price Animation**: Smooth transitions when quantities change
- **Loading States**: Skeleton loaders during updates
- **Toast Notifications**: "Item removed", "Coupon applied"
- **Sticky Summary**: Order summary stays visible on scroll

### Mobile View
- Single column layout
- Fixed bottom bar with total and checkout button
- Swipe to remove items
- Collapsible order summary

### Colors & Theme
- Maintain terracotta (#B8734C) for CTAs
- Cream backgrounds (#F5E6D3)
- Success green for savings/discounts
- Subtle animations for all interactions
